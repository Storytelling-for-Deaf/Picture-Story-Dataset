01- Once, a dog was searching for food. It got a bone from a meat shop.
02- The dog took the bone and went towards its house. It reached the bridge of the river which was near his house. 
03- The dog saw his image in the water and mistook it for some other dog is there in the water with a bone in its mouth. 
04- The greedy dog made a plan to snatch away the bone from the other dog. It opened its mouth to snatch away the bone. The bone in its mouth fell into the river.
05- The poor dog lost its bone and went home crying.
