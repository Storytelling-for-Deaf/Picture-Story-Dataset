01- Once upon a time, four cows lived in the village near the forest. They were very close friends who stayed together all day.
02- They graze the fields and roam the forest together.
03- Once the lion in the forest saw them, the lion tried to hunt and eat them.
04- When the four cows were grazing together the lion attacked them.
05- The cows together attacked the lion and chased him away.
06- After a few days, the four cows quarreled among themselves and started to graze separately. 
07- The lion took advantage of this situation. When the cows were alone, the lion succeeded in hunting them down. One by one, the lion killed them and ate them.
