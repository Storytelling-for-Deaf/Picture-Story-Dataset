01- One day a cunning fox went to the village to find food.
02- The fox saw a chicken coop with its door open and came near it.
03- Suddenly two dogs in the farm saw the fox and started chasing it.
04- While running, the fox fell into a large basin full of blue paint.
05- The fox thought to take advantage of the change in color and screamed with a dangerous voice to scare the wild animals as a new animal.
06- Knowing that this was the cunning fox from before, the animals chased the fox out of the forest.
