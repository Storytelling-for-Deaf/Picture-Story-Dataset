01-One day a crow was hungry and flew over the trees looking for something to eat.
02-The crow found a piece of cheese. He took the piece of cheese and went up a tall tree.
03-A fox that had been hungry all day passed by here.
04-The fox saw the crow getting ready to eat the cheese.
05- The fox thought of a way to get this cheese piece.
06- The fox said to the crow, "Oh crow, your voice is very sweet. I would like to hear a song from that beautiful voice." 
07-The crow was deceived by the fox's words. The crow opened its mouth to sing. With that, the cheese piece fell to the ground.
08- The cunning fox laughed and enjoyed the piece of cheese.
